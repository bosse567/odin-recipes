So you need a boost ? Let's go !

Good looking smoothies

It can be either before or during a good workout, but you need some energy to use all your strength. This smoothie is the perfect way to train hard!

Ingredients

  • skyr or yogurt
  • cinnamon
  • fruits : mango, banana, kiwi, apple, pineapple, berries...
  • On top : chocolate, whole fruits...

Steps

  1. First of all, take a shaker and put your milk product with the fruits and add some cinnamon if wanted.
  2. Shake it with a blender, and put the mixture in a glass.
  3. Put what you want on the top.
  4. Enjoy your drink!

If you want to add proteins to your smoothie, add some peanut butter, or more efficient, add a bit of whey powder. Be careful with whey, ask first a doctor.
