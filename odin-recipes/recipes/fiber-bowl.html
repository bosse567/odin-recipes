Who needs a high fiber meal ?

Here's an example of a bowl filled with good fiber

These types of bowls are very interesting for your muscles, especially for their recovery and strength.

Made of yogurt, greek yogurt or skyr, they also have plenty of proteins, added also by the chia seeds or the nuts.

These bowls are better with fruits, don't hesitate to add plenty of them.

Ingredients

Here are some ideas of ingredients that I would use to make my bowl. If you have other ideas, please try them !

  • yogurt or skyr(there is a lot of flavour possible, try plain or mangos!)
  • chia seeds
  • nuts : cashew, brazilian, hazelnut, almonds...
  • peanut butter
  • dark chocolate
  • oats
  • fruits : mangos, bananas, blueberries, strawberries...

Now that you have an idea, let's cook!

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, put either yogurt or skyr or any milk product.
  2. After soaking chia seeds in milk or water for a few hours, add them on a side of your bowl.
  3. On the other side, add some peanut butter, and on top of it two spoons of oats.
  4. Then add the tops you like : nuts, chocolate, fruits, cinnamon...

Congratulations, you just did an amazing, healthy breakfast bowl! Enjoy it in the morning, before or after a workout!
