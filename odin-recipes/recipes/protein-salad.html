How to make a good protein salad ?

A chickpeas and kale salad.

This salad is filled with fibers and protein, super healthy and perfect for an athlete's nutrition.

Don't forget vegetables, give them a try ! If you want to build strong, healthy muscles, you need fibers !

Here's how to create one of those healthy salads:

Ingredients

This list gives you an idea of ingredients you need, but you can of course add as many as you want.

  • chickpeas
  • kale
  • boiled eggs
  • a half avocado
  • quinoa
  • lentils
  • olive oil
  • fruits:mangos, kiwis...

Steps

Please follow precisely the following steps, this is an idea to create my favorite salad, but you can add or remove whatever you want.

  1. First, wash the chicpeas, the quinoa, the lentils and the kale.
  2. Boil two or more eggs, without overcooking them.
  3. Chop the fruits, the vegetables and cut the avocado.
  4. Cook the quinoa and the lentils in a low temperature, let them boil gently.
  5. In a medium bowl, put the quinoa, add the lentils on top of it, then the chickpeas and the kale.
  6. Once your base finished, add the eggs, the avocado and the fruits. If wanted, you can add some nuts.
  7. And finally, enjoy !
